Soil Sample Submission Form

Required Materials:

- Phone (or compass)
- Mason Jar with lid
- Metal spoon (or stick)
- Lables (or tape)
- Sharpie for labeling the sample
- Paper to mark location

Optional Materials:

- Gloves
- Trash Bags
- Bug Spray
- Sunblock

How to collect Soil Sample Video

How to Collect Soil Sample

Duration:
Sample Collection: 10-15 minutes
Clean Up: 5-10 minutes

Sample collections will be done by using a stick or metal spoon to dig out some soil, wanting the hole to be about 6 inches deep (or about the length of a sharpie or pen) into the soil.
After the hole is made collect the soil from the bottom of the hole and place into into the glass jar, then label the jar with the sample location.

Multipule samples will be labeled
ex: A1, A2 ...

Sample Clean-Up

Once finished make sure to pick up any trash that you may have brought with you and take anything you brought with you back.
This is where the gloves and trash bag would come in if you wanted to collect the trash that is in the location you are collecting your soil sample from.
